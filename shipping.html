Shipping

Usually if your order was placed before 1pm, working days, we are able to ship it the very same day. Otherwise your order will be sent out on the next business day. It might take us up to 5 business days to ship your order during sale or Latvian holyday periods. If it’s really urgent, feel free to hit us up at +371 6767 1505.

All shipments are fully insured and our policy is to give you a full refund or to send a replacement if your parcel get lost. Please contact us if so.

For orders with more than one pair of shoes you will be automatically charged €20 (DHL Expres) / €10 (DPD) for every extra pair.

For decks (skateboarding) orders you will be automatically charged €20 (DHL Expres) / €10 (DPD) for every extra unit.

We ship the order according the shipping address and name provided by you when plans the order. Be aware that your ID might be requested to receive it.

We are happy to offer you FREE SHIPPING on all domestic region orders (full priced items) over Eur 100 for Latvia, Lithuania and Estonia. It will be applied automatically during checkout. Free shipping is only available for orders with one pair of shoes or deck, for each additional pair/deck you will be automatically charged €10.

If the order is not received by no fault of the delivery service, itk reserves the right to deduct any costs associated with returning. Please track your order with the tracking number provided to you. If for some reason you cannot receive the order, please contact us in advance via info@itkkit.com.

In the case that an address cannot be reached by the selected transport company, itk store reserves the right to change the delivery service.
